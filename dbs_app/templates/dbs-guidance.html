Criminal Record (DBS) Check

You will need to apply for a criminal record (DBS) check.

This must be an enhanced check for a home-based childcare role.

It can take about 6 weeks to get your certificate.

We will accept an existing enhanced check if one of the following apply:

  • the certificate is dated within the last 3 months
  • you are signed up to the DBS update service.

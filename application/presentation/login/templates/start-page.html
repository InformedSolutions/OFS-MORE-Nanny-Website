Register as a nanny (England)

Register with Ofsted as a nanny or au pair to look after children in their own home.

Before you start

You will need the following:

  • UK criminal record certificate
  • first aid training for the age group you'll look after
  • childcare training - speak to your local council
  • public liability insurance

If you've lived abroad in the last 5 years, you'll need certificates of good conduct from those countries.

How long it takes

It should take 15 minutes to fill in this form.

It usually takes up to 12 weeks to register with Ofsted.

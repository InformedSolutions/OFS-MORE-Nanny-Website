What's next

Ofsted will:

  • review your application again
  • ask for references
  • carry out background checks with other organisations
  • check the health declaration booklet
  • arrange for an inspector to visit

All this usually takes up to 12 weeks.

There can be delays if information is missing or we have to wait for references or background checks.

Once all the checks have been done, an inspector will call you to arrange a date when they can visit.

The inspector will tell you their recommendation on the day of the visit.
